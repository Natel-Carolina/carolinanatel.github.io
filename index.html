About Me

I am phD. student in Water Resources and Environmental Engineering at Federal University of Paraná. Currently guest student at University of Zurich (Department of Geography).
I am interested in research related to climate change impacts on water resources, uncertainties, Machine Learning/Deep Learning methods in Hydrology and Bias correction technics.
